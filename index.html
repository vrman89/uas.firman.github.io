About Us

Ricwil incorporated was originally founded in USA by two engineers, Richard dan William in the year 1902 for the purpose of developing and manufacturing of pre – insulated pipes. After giving its best efforts, Ricwil was then nationally recognized.

In the middle of 21st century, Ricwil expand to Japan in 1974 and then to Malaysia in 1984. Because of the great market in Indonesia, Ricwil Malaysia decided to expand its growth to this country. Thus, Ricwil Indonesia was formed in 2005. Since then, PT Ricwil Indonesia has been living up to its name of producing high quality pre – insulated and site – insulation pipes, as well as, providing best services which brings satisfaction to its customers. We also provide complete thermal insulation and pipe work installation services where we offer engineering, design and supervision.
